ClearClips Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

ClearClips ("we", "our", "us") is a content creation tool operated by ClearOpsAI. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services, including our TikTok integration.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ClearClips with TikTok integration, we may access:

  • Your TikTok account profile information (username, display name)
  • Permission to post content to your TikTok account on your behalf

We do not collect, store, or share your TikTok password. Authentication is handled securely through TikTok's OAuth system.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Post video clips to your TikTok account as authorized by you
  • Track posting status and basic performance metrics

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely on our local infrastructure. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. All data processing happens locally on our secure servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your authentication data only as long as you maintain an active connection with our service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Access information about what data we have collected

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: privacy@clearopsai.io
