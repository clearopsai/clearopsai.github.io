Your System

5-step weekly lead gen plan

Block these into your calendar. About 5 hours per week total.

Monday
60 min
Batch 3 pieces of content
Use Canva + CapCut (free). 1 Reel (Herndon neighborhood or "homes under $700K"), 1 Carousel (market data or investment tip), 1 Story (poll, Q&A, or behind-the-scenes). Hook in the first 3 seconds. Location-tag everything.
Tue + Thu
30 min each
Engage 20 local accounts
Find NoVA agents, lenders, stagers, local businesses. Like 2 recent posts, leave 1 genuine comment, follow accounts in the 1K to 5K range. Search #HerndonVA · #DMVRealEstate · #NoVARealtor. People who comment on top agents' posts are your future buyers.
Wednesday
45 min
Sphere of influence outreach
Contact 10 people. Personal texts only. Text 5 past clients: "Hey [name], just saw a home sell on your street for $X. Want me to pull your numbers?" DM 5 friends: "Know anyone buying or selling in Herndon? I'm taking on a few new clients." This is where 61% of all agent business comes from.
Friday
30 min
Google Business + Nextdoor
Google: Post 1 update, add 1 to 2 photos, text 1 past client for a review. 73% of buyers only trust reviews from the last month. Nextdoor: Answer 2 to 3 neighborhood questions. Share 1 market insight. Be helpful, never pitch.
Sunday
30 min
Story poll + DM follow-up
Post a Story poll: "Thinking about buying or selling in Herndon in 2026?" Instagram shows you who tapped Yes. DM each one within the hour. Review your inbox and reply to anyone who engaged this week.
