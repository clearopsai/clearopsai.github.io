How we calculated your score
Your Clarity Score is calculated from the operational gaps identified in your action plan. Each gap is weighted by its severity (estimated hours/week of team time consumed) and mapped to one of four dimensions: Process Visibility, Response Speed, Data Accuracy, and Team Alignment.

Dimension baselines are derived from industry benchmarks across 200+ organizations in your sector. Your score represents how much operational visibility your team currently has compared to organizations with fully connected toolchains. A higher score means fewer blind spots.
